FOODle

How to play

Guess the FOODle in 6 tries.

Pick a category you wanted to make your guess and you're ready to go!

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word
  • The colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word
  • There are no double letters in the answer

Examples


  • M is in the word and in the correct spot

  • L is in the word and but in the wrong spot

  • All the letters in your guess in not in the word

If you want to try again, simply refresh the page or click the Reset button and select the theme again!
